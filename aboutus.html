About AnchorX

Who We Are | Our Mission

AnchorX is a unblocked website type project made by one 13 year old developer. AnchorX was founded in 2022, and is an expanding games website. You may notice that AnchorX isn't the same as all the other unblocked sites, as all the others are made on google sites. I made this website on Visual Studio Code in two days for a challenging project, and to help students and workers enjoy their lives when working. Thanks for reading!
